Our Story

From Highland Farms to Your Cup

Founded in 2010, Highland Brew sources only the highest quality coffee beans from sustainable farms across the world's premier coffee-growing regions.

Our master roasters use traditional techniques combined with modern technology to bring out the unique character of each bean.

Every batch is carefully tested to ensure consistency and quality in every cup you brew.
